## Strategic Objective

Sales data represents an organization's most valuable operational asset, yet raw transactional records yield limited strategic insight. This BI asset transforms six years of fragmented sales data into a unified intelligence layer enabling:

- **Executive Decision Support:** Real-time visibility into revenue health, regional performance, and customer segment dynamics.
- **Profitability Intelligence:** Multi-dimensional analysis across product categories, geographic territories, and temporal trends.
- **Strategic Planning Foundation:** Historical pattern synthesis enabling evidence-based forecasting and resource allocation.

The asset is deployed on both Power BI and Tableau, demonstrating cross-platform BI engineering capability and providing redundancy for enterprise stakeholder preferences.

## Key Performance Indicators

### Executive KPIs

| Metric | Definition | Strategic Value |
|---|---|---|
| Total Revenue | Gross sales volume (current period) | Overall business health |
| Revenue Growth | YoY / QoQ percentage change | Trajectory assessment |
| Gross Margin | Revenue minus cost of goods sold | Profitability efficiency |
| Regional Profitability | Margin contribution by territory | Resource allocation optimization |
| Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) | Projected future revenue per customer | Retention investment decisions |

### Operational KPIs

| Metric | Definition | Tactical Application |
|---|---|---|
| Average Order Value (AOV) | Revenue per transaction | Pricing strategy evaluation |
| Sales Cycle Duration | Lead-to-close timeline | Pipeline efficiency assessment |
| Win Rate | Closed-won / total opportunities | Competitive positioning analysis |
| Regional Penetration | Market share by territory | Expansion opportunity identification |
